Help [show][hide]

A SMILES string is a way of representing a molecule.

Option 1: Enter strings by hand. Examples SMILES for you to try include:

  • C=CCCCC
  • C1C=CCC1
  • ClC=CBr
  • CC(CO)CC(=O)C

Option 2: Download and launch JChemPaint, draw a molecule, and go to Tools > Create SMILES. Then, paste that SMILES here.

Option 3: Download and launch MarvinSketch, draw a molecule, and go to Edit > Source > View (in popup) > SMILES > SMILES. Then, paste that SMILES here.
